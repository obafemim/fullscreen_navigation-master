Our Story

Muse has a funny history. When Emmanuel and I decided to start our own retail business in 2014 we knew that it would be tough to find the right platform for our needs. Both of us have incredibly high standards when it comes to aesthetics and simplicity. Unfortunately, those standards were not found with the service providers in the digital agency industry. We quickly developed our own platform to power our retail store, and it ran successfully for a season.

So, there we were, with this really cool platform and a retail business which had little hope of supporting us in the summer months. Options were weighed, a year and a bit later we had Muse Agency which for all intents and purposes was exactly the platform we tried to find when we incorporated our own business back in 2014. It validates everything we set out to do in the last 2 and a half years: to provide the best designed full-service digital agency system on the planet.
